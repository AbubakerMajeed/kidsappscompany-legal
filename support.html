Common Questions

  • Is the app safe for kids? Yes. We collect no personal information from children. See our Privacy Policies.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Open the app, go to the purchase screen, and tap "Restore Purchases". Purchases are tied to your Apple account.
  • How do I turn off ads? Ads are non-personalized and family-safe. Where available, an in-app purchase removes them.
  • How do I request a refund? Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
