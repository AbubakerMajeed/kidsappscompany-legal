Privacy Policy — Number Trace

App: Number Trace (com.kidsappscompany.numbertrace1000) · Publisher: Kids Apps Company · Last updated: June 12, 2026

Number Trace ("the App") is made for young children and is designed to be safe by default. We do not collect personal information from children. This policy explains what limited, non-personal data the App processes and how it is used. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses.
  • No photos, videos, or voice recordings.
  • No precise location.
  • No device advertising identifier (IDFA) from a child, and no account or login for a child.

Information We Process

To understand how lessons are used and to keep the App working, we process a small amount of anonymous, non-personal data:

  • Anonymous usage analytics — e.g. which lesson was completed or where a flow breaks. Each install is identified only by an app-generated, anonymous install ID. Property values are bounded categories or numbers and never free text that could carry personal data. Processed via Google Firebase and Mixpanel in their no-advertising-identifier / families mode.
  • Crash and performance diagnostics — to fix bugs and improve stability.

Advertising

The App may show ads served by Google AdMob. All ads are non-personalized and run in Google's families / child-directed mode, meaning ads are not based on a user's profile and no advertising identifier is used for tracking. Learn more at Google AdMob & families.

In-App Purchases

The App may offer optional in-app purchases. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store and are tied to the parent's or guardian's Apple account — never to a child profile. We do not receive or store payment card details.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Privacy & Security
  • Google AdMob — Advertising policies
  • Mixpanel — Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — Apple Privacy

Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

The App is intended for children and complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent rules. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because we only use anonymous, non-personal data and non-personalized ads, no parental consent is required for personal data collection — there is none to collect.

Data Retention & Security

Anonymous analytics are retained only as long as needed to understand product health and are not linked to any individual. We use industry-standard safeguards to protect the data we process.

Your Rights

If you have questions or wish to request deletion of any anonymous data associated with your install, contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
